TOTI

Cosmetic Packaging Design, Branding

PROJECT BRIEF

Design and output a range of campaign items for a cosmetic company to promote its new Rehydra product range, which focuses on women with dry and dehydrated skin. The idea is every product range should use a colour which represents a type of skin. Produce product packaging, logo, promotional flyer and brochure to attract health conscience women who want professional and natural beauty products. They want to market the product internationally. Have to source/create all of the content and photograph for final products.

CHALLENGE

Develop and design a cosmetic range which gives the audience extra health benefits. Creating dyelines for the packaging. Developing cosmetic containers style and size. Give names for each product items to match the desired style. Creating content for the range. Photograph product items, set up studio lights and create interesting imagery, edit photos.

SOLUTION

Developed and designed a simple and clean brand. The product range created for internal and external treatment together. The blue colour represents freshness, water and hydration. The logo is a simple typeface with an elegant touch. The letter 'O' represents harmony and balance. Plant illustrations were created to provide a natural look. Inside every package a message is provided to tell a story about each product. The promotional items provide information about the company philosophy and the Rehydra line to educate the audience and promote the product. Every package has a number for step by step usage. The product photos style is clean and consistence.
