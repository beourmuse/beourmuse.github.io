KARMER

Editorial Design, Magazine

PROJECT BRIEF

Created a contemporary magazine focusing on fashion, beauty and culture. The magazine contain an article, an interview, one half and one full page ad and information that appeals to the target audience

CHALLENGE

Sourcing and creating the magazine text content and product photos. Finding the models and styling, and applying make-up on them. Setting up the photo studio lights, directing the model photo shoot. Edit and retouch photos for the desired magazine style.

SOLUTION

Created a consistent layout style by using white spaces, photos and patterns on each page. Used different text colours like red to attract the audience on the cover page. Added trendy typography to make the layout more interesting. The articles sourced the most popular and latest products in beauty and fashion so women can learn about the new trends. Using feminine photography to make women feel confident in what they wear.
